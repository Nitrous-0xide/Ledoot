Lockheed Martin's FGM-148 JAVELIN


The FGM-148 Javelin, or Advanced Anti-Tank Weapon System-Medium, is an American-made man-portable anti-tank system in service since 1996 and continuously upgraded. It replaced the M47 Dragon anti-tank missile in US service.


Designed by: Texas Instruments & Martin Marietta, now Raytheon Technologies & Lockheed Martin

SPECIFICATIONS

Range: 65m to 4,000m in most operational conditions Warhead: Tandem-shaped charge, multi-purpose warhead Magnification: 4x for day sight 4x or 12x for thermal sight Weight: <34.1 lb (15.5 kg) all-up round Length: 47.2 in (119.8 cm) launch tube Diameter: 5.6 in (14.2 cm) launch tube Readiness Rate: >99% availability Reliability: >94% engagement success rate
